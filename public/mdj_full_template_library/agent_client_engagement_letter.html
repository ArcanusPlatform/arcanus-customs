Letter of Engagement for Customs Consultancy Services

Thank you for choosing {{agent.company}} to assist with your customs duty repayment and compliance matters. This letter outlines the terms of our engagement and the services we will provide.

1. Engagement Overview

We are appointed as your customs consultants and agents for:

  • Preparing and submitting C285 repayment claims to HMRC
  • Managing customs compliance and advisory services
  • Representing you in communications with HMRC
  • Providing expert guidance on customs duty, VAT, and trade matters

2. Scope of Services

2.1 C285 Repayment Claims

  • Review of import declarations
  • Preparation of C285 repayment applications
  • Calculation of duty, VAT, and excise overpayments
  • Compilation of supporting evidence
  • Submission and monitoring of claims
  • Handling HMRC queries and correspondence

2.2 Customs Compliance Advisory

  • Tariff classification guidance
  • Customs valuation advice
  • Origin and preference determination
  • Import/export procedural guidance
  • Compliance audits and risk assessments

2.3 Representation and Communication

  • Acting as your authorised representative with HMRC
  • Managing all HMRC correspondence
  • Responding to information requests
  • Negotiating where appropriate

3. Client Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Supply required documents promptly
  • Maintain required customs records
  • Notify us of changes to your business or circumstances
  • Review and approve documents prior to submission
  • Forward any HMRC correspondence to us immediately

4. Fees and Payment Terms

4.1 Fee Structure

  • Success Fee: 25% of the recovered amount (ex VAT)
  • No Win, No Fee: No charge if unsuccessful
  • Minimum Fee: £500 per successful claim

4.2 Payment Terms

  • Success fees payable within 14 days of refund receipt
  • Hourly advisory services billed monthly
  • Payment due within 30 days

4.3 Expenses

Out-of-pocket expenses may be recharged. Approval obtained for items over £100.

5. Confidentiality and Data Protection

  • All information is treated as confidential
  • Data processed in compliance with UK GDPR
  • We maintain appropriate security controls

6. Professional Standards and Liability

6.1 Standards

  • HMRC regulations
  • UK customs law and UCC
  • Professional ethics and conduct

6.2 Liability

  • Liability capped at fees paid or £100,000
  • PI insurance of £1,000,000 maintained

7. Duration and Termination

  • Starts on date of signature
  • Continues until services complete
  • Either party may terminate with 30 days' notice
