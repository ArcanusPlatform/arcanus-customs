Scope of Authorization

to act as our authorized declarant for customs purposes, with authority to:

  1. Make Customs Declarations: Submit import and export declarations to HMRC on our behalf using the Customs Declaration Service (CDS) or other approved systems.
  2. Represent Our Interests: Act as our representative in all matters relating to customs declarations, including providing information and responding to HMRC queries.
  3. Access Our EORI: Use our EORI number ({{trader.eori}}) when making declarations on our behalf.
  4. Submit Documentation: Provide supporting documentation, evidence, and information to HMRC as required for customs clearance.
  5. Receive Communications: Receive correspondence, notifications, and decisions from HMRC relating to declarations made on our behalf.
  6. Make Amendments: Submit amendments, corrections, or supplementary declarations as necessary.
  7. Handle Post-Clearance Matters: Manage post-clearance amendments, C285 claims, and other customs compliance matters.

Declaration Types Covered

This authorization covers the following types of customs declarations:

  • ☑ Import Declarations (H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6)
  • ☑ Export Declarations (C21, D21)
  • ☑ Simplified Declarations
  • ☑ Supplementary Declarations
  • ☑ Post-Clearance Amendments
  • ☑ C285 Repayment Claims
  • ☑ Other customs procedures as required

Trader Responsibilities

We confirm that:

  • We will provide the declarant with accurate and complete information for all declarations
  • We remain ultimately responsible for the accuracy of all declarations made on our behalf
  • We will ensure the declarant has access to all necessary documentation
  • We will notify HMRC immediately if this authorization is revoked
  • We understand that we are liable for any duties, taxes, and penalties arising from declarations

Declarant Responsibilities

The declarant confirms that they will:

  • Make declarations in accordance with customs regulations and HMRC requirements
  • Use reasonable care to ensure the accuracy of all declarations
  • Maintain proper records in accordance with customs record-keeping requirements
  • Notify the trader of any issues, queries, or decisions from HMRC
  • Act in the trader's best interests at all times

Data Protection and Confidentiality

We authorize HMRC to share information relating to our customs declarations and compliance matters with the named declarant. We understand that the declarant is bound by professional confidentiality obligations and data protection requirements.

We confirm that the declarant has our consent to process our personal and business data for customs declaration purposes in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Duration of Authorization

This authorization shall remain in force until:

  • Written notice of revocation is provided to HMRC by either party, or
  • A period of 12 months from the date of this letter, whichever occurs first

This authorization may be renewed by written confirmation from both parties.
